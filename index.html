Eco-Friendly Tips

Reduce, reuse, and recycle materials whenever possible.

Use energy-efficient appliances and light bulbs.

Plant trees to absorb CO2 and provide shade.

Use public transport or carpool to reduce emissions.

Avoid single-use plastics and opt for reusable alternatives.

Support local and sustainable businesses.

Conserve water by fixing leaks and turning off taps when not in use.
